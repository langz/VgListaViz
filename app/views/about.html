Danceability

We each groove to different music; and what constitutes dance music is inherently subjective. The Echo Nest is representing how danceable a song is by a number that ranges from 0 to 1. It is used a mix of features to compute danceability, including beat strength, tempo stability, overall tempo, and more.

Energy

A number that ranges from 0 to 1, representing how energetic The Echo Nest thinks this song is. How energetic is the music? Does it make you want to bop all over the room, or fall into a coma? Compared to danceability this is less subjective. The feature mix used to compute energy includes loudness and segment durations.

Key

The key that The Echo Nest believes the song is in. Key signatures start at 0 (C) and ascend the chromatic scale. In this case, a key of 1 represents a song in D-flat.

Mode

Number representing whether the song is in a minor (0) or major (1) key. Use this in conjunction with 'key'.

Time Signature

Time signature of the key; how many beats per measure.

Tempo

Represents the Beats per Minute(BPM) of a song.

Duration

Length of the song, in seconds.

Loudness

The overall loudness of a track in decibels (dB).
